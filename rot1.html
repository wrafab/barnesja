My Approach



My approach to this class is to jump into the project with whatever instruction Mr. Gerber gives me. I will probably

fail, at which point I will look up a video or google a solution to my problem.

This was a challenge where our team was given tape, string, straws, and a cup and we were told to suspend

the cup using the string. The goal was to hold as many pennies as we could. We held the second most of

the groups that were not disqualified. The design failed, however, because of our lack of triangles and

the fact that it was too tall and not compact. My contributions to the project were to suggest the idea

of placing one straw inside another to link them together
